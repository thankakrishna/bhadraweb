🕉️ Kanjampuram Heritage

The Sacred Abode of Bhadreshwari Amman

Nestled in the spiritual heartland of Kanjampuram, Kanyakumari District, Arulmigu Bhadreshwari Amman Kovil stands as a beacon of divine grace. The Goddess Bhadreshwari — an embodiment of Adi Parasakthi — showers her devotees with protection, prosperity, and eternal blessings.

"Where the divine mother resides, there blooms eternal peace. Kanjampuram is sanctified by her sacred presence, protecting all devotees who seek her refuge."

Managed by Samrakshana Seva Trust (Reg. 179/2004), the temple serves as a center for spiritual worship, cultural preservation, and community welfare.

Sacred Manthirams

Bhadreshwari Moola Manthiram

ॐ ऐं ह्रीं श्रीं
भद्रेश्वरि देव्यै नमः
ॐ शक्ति ॐ

Chant 108 times daily for divine protection and prosperity

Amman Gayatri Manthiram

ॐ महादेव्यै विद्महे
भद्रकाल्यै धीमहि
तन्नो देवी प्रचोदयात्

For divine wisdom and spiritual awakening

Durga Beej Manthiram

ॐ दुं दुर्गायै नमः
ॐ ऐं ह्रीं क्लीं
चामुण्डायै विच्चे

Powerful protection mantra for removing obstacles
