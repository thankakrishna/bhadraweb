Institutes & Services

எங்கள் நிறுவனங்கள் — Service to Society

Vidya Dhanam — Education

Comprehensive educational support program empowering underprivileged students in the Kanjampuram region with quality education and career guidance.

  • Free tuition center for classes 1–12
  • College scholarships for meritorious students
  • Computer literacy & digital skills programs
  • Annual talent & sports competitions
  • Career counseling & placement assistance
  • Free textbooks & stationery distribution

Anna Dhanam — Free Food

Blessed food distribution program serving nutritious meals to devotees, travelers, elderly, and underprivileged families every day at the temple.

  • Daily free meals at temple premises
  • Festival special Annadhanam (1000+ meals)
  • Senior citizen & widow meal programs
  • Emergency food relief during calamities
  • Nutritious mid-day meals for students
  • Prasadam distribution on all festival days

Health & Wellness

Free healthcare services bringing medical care to doorsteps of the local community with regular health camps and wellness programs.

  • Monthly free medical camps
  • Eye care & dental check-up camps
  • Free medicine distribution for elderly
  • Yoga & meditation classes (daily)
  • Blood donation drives (quarterly)
  • Ayurveda & Siddha awareness programs

Cultural Academy

Preserving and promoting South Indian classical arts, temple culture, and traditional knowledge through structured training by expert teachers.

  • Carnatic vocal & instrumental music
  • Bharatanatyam & folk dance training
  • Vedic chanting & Sanskrit classes
  • Nadaswaram & Thavil training
  • Annual cultural festival & competitions
  • Temple arts documentation project
🌳

Go Seva & Environment

Cow shelter (Goshala), tree planting drives, temple garden maintenance, and eco-friendly festival practices.

👰

Mass Marriage Program

Annual free mass marriage ceremony for underprivileged families with complete wedding arrangements and blessings.

🧓

Elder Care

Visiting and supporting elderly community members with food, medicine, clothing, and spiritual companionship.
