Daily Pooja Schedule

தினசரி பூஜை நேரம் — Sacred Rituals

🌅

Usha Kala Pooja

5:30 AM — 6:30 AM

Dawn worship with Suprabatham recital, Abhishekam with holy water, milk, turmeric, kumkum, and sandal paste. The Goddess is awakened with sacred chants.

☀️

Kalai Pooja

8:00 AM — 9:00 AM

Morning Pooja with elaborate Alankaram, offering of fresh flowers, fruits, and special Naivedyam. Goddess adorned in silk and divine jewelry.

🔆

Uchikala Pooja

12:00 PM — 12:30 PM

Noon worship with Annadhanam offering. Grand Deepa Aradhana with 108 oil lamps illuminating the sanctum sanctorum.

🌆

Sayaratchai Pooja

5:30 PM — 6:30 PM

Evening worship with Deeparadhana, devotional music, camphor lighting, and Thiru Neeru distribution to devotees.

🌙

Arthajama Pooja

8:00 PM — 8:30 PM

Final pooja — Goddess offered Paal naivedyam and lullaby hymns. The divine energy envelops the temple as doors close.

🔥

Special Archanas

Available All Day

Sahasranama Archana, Kumkum Archana, Thulasi Archana, Pushpa Archana, and Vilakku Pooja. Book for family welfare.
