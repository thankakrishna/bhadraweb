Annual Festivals

திருவிழாக்கள் — Divine Celebrations

🌺 Chithirai Peruvizha

April — May (Chithirai)

Grand 10-day annual festival with elaborate decorations, Theruvizha (Chariot Festival), cultural programs, Kumbabishekam rituals, and special Homams. Thousands gather from across Tamil Nadu.

🔥 Aadi Perukku & Aadi Pooram

July — August (Aadi)

Sacred Aadi month celebrations honoring the Goddess. Special Abhishekams, Kumkum Archana with 1008 names, and grand Annadhanam for all devotees.

🪔 Navaratri Vizha

September — October (Purattasi)

Nine nights celebrating nine forms of Shakti. Golu display, Saraswati Pooja, Vijayadashami, cultural performances, Bommai Golu competition, and community feasts.

🕯️ Karthigai Deepam

November — December (Karthigai)

Festival of Lights — entire temple illuminated with thousands of oil lamps. Maha Deepam ceremony, community lighting, and special Annadhanam.

✨ Thai Poosam & Pongal

January (Thai)

Harvest thanksgiving with Surya Pooja, Kavadi Attam, traditional celebrations with music and dance, and special Annadhanam.

🌙 Panguni Uthiram

March — April (Panguni)

Divine celestial marriage festival. Elaborate wedding rituals, Pallakku Varisai, Veda Parayanam, and sacred Prasadam distribution.

🌕 Fridays & Full Moon

Every Friday & Pournami

Special weekly Friday Pooja with Vilakku Pooja and Kumkum Archana. Full Moon days feature grand Deeparadhana and Bhajan sessions.
